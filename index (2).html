Two decisions that actually cost real money — lock these in first

Laptop: Windows, non-negotiable. VMI IT does not support macOS or Chromebooks. Minimum 16GB RAM / 512GB SSD for most majors, 32GB/1TB for Engineering majors. Cyber Security isn't explicitly listed in either tier — email help@vmi.edu to confirm before you buy. If your dev workflow lives on a Mac, this is a real toolchain shift, budget the time to rebuild it, not just the money for the machine.

Boots: one pair, from a locked list of four models. Belleville C390, FC390 (women's), Khyber II TR550, C320, FC320, Sabre C333 — nothing else. Arrive with unauthorized boots (including anything with USMC markings or zippers) and you'll be sent to the Military Store to buy the right pair anyway. Order early enough to break them in before you fly.

Don't waste money on these — confiscated or banned

Confiscated on sight

  • Weapons or knives of any type
  • Microwaves, hot pots, heating elements, any appliance
  • Desk fan larger than 12"
  • Plants, pets
  • Candles, fireworks, incense — anything flammable
  • TVs or any entertainment media
  • Computer monitor over 32" diagonal
  • Alcohol, drugs, paraphernalia

Banned until Breakout (spring)

  • Electric razors
  • DVDs, CDs, computer games
  • iPods, MP3 players, computer speakers
  • Any clothing/linens with visible logos or emblems
  • Transition-lens eyeglasses
  • Smart watches (social media/email/text capable)
  • Cell phone — stays with your dyke until Breakout
